Communication Definition : (Write around 80 -100 words). Communication is the process of exchanging ideas, information, and feelings through various methods, including verbal, non-verbal, and written forms. Effective communication involves not only the transmission of a message but also active listening and appropriate feedback to ensure mutual understanding. It is essential for building relationships, resolving conflicts, and fostering collaboration in both personal and professional environments. Good communication requires clarity, empathy, and adaptability to different contexts and audiences, making it a critical skill in all aspects of life.
