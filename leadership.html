Leadership

Leadership Definition: (Write around 80 -100 words). Leadership is the ability to inspire, guide, and influence a group toward achieving shared goals. It involves creating a vision, setting clear objectives, and empowering others to take initiative while fostering collaboration and trust. Effective leadership is not tied to formal authority but stems from qualities such as integrity, authenticity, and emotional intelligence. A good leader also adapts to change, motivates individuals to reach their full potential, and creates an environment where innovation and growth can thrive, ultimately leading to producing quality results and achieving lasting success.
